ABOUT US

Founded in 2019, Dayal Fabricator has grown into a trusted name in the manufacturing of high-quality uPVC doors and windows. Driven by a passionate team of engineers and experts, we are committed to creating eco-friendly, sustainable, and innovative solutions that redefine modern living and working spaces. Our products are designed with European standards in mind, ensuring unmatched durability, strength, and long-lasting performance. Each frame is manufactured using superior-grade uPVC profiles and cutting-edge technology at our state-of-the-art production facility, guaranteeing precision and consistency in every product we deliver.

What sets us apart is our commitment to sustainability. By offering uPVC solutions that reduce energy consumption through advanced heat and sound insulation, we help customers lower energy costs while enjoying enhanced comfort all year round. Our doors and windows are also maintenance-free, termite-resistant, and weather-proof, making them a smart investment for both residential and commercial spaces. At Dayal Fabricator, we combine innovation, craftsmanship, and advanced technology to create products that not only improve the functionality and aesthetics of your property but also contribute to a greener and more sustainable future.

OUR VISION AND MISSION

To become a trusted leader in sustainable uPVC door and window solutions, transforming every home and workspace with innovation, durability, and eco-friendly design. We envision a future where modern living and environmental responsibility go hand in hand, delivering products that enhance comfort, reduce energy consumption, and set new benchmarks in quality and performance.
